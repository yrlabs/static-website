Market Ratio
Dimensions variable. Mixed media installation

About

Market Ratio brings together multiple works in dialogue, examining the shifting dynamics of value, abstraction, and perception within contemporary economies. Rational Fantasy, depicted on the right, isolates a single work from the installation, highlighting the tension between collective systems and individual perspectives. The pairing explores how artistic forms can embody both structural logic and imaginative speculation.
